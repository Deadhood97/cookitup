## ğŸ® How to Play

Use your **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** to slide all tiles on the grid.
- **Combine**: Slide two identical ingredients together to merge them (e.g., `Flour + Water = Dough`).
- **Transform**: Interact with stationary tools or elements (e.g., `Dough + Fire = Bread`).
- **Goal**: Complete the specific recipes required for each level before you run out of moves or space!
